• High School Transcripts/GED Scores
    • Must be official and have:
      • All Pages
      • All Years
      • Full School Name
      • Scores
      • Full Graduation Date
      • Degree Type
      • Full Legal Name
    • Potential 3rd Parties you can research besides the High School itself (3rd Party may require a fee. Full Sail University is not responsible for the cost and must be handled by the student themselves):
      • Parchment
      • GED
      • Department of Education State Contacts
    • Cannot be edited or editable.
      • If this is found to have occurred, documents may need to be sent directly to Full Sail University w/notations to keep official for review.
    • Sometimes High School Diplomas can be reviewed.
      • There is the potential for transcripts being requested still after the diploma is reviewed if more info is needed.
    • Likewise, if you’ve graduated with an Associates or higher from an accredited college, sometimes the official college transcripts can be reviewed in lieu of high school equivalents, as well.
    • Can be uploaded to the updated Full Sail One App (Apple Store or Google Play) or the Full Sail One Website for review.

  • Technology Readiness Assessment
    • All necessary components must accessed and completed on a desktop, laptop, or tablet on the Full Sail One Website.
    • Must get a passing score.
      • 0%-69% = If this score is received on the 1st Attempt, a Tech Prep Workshop will be provided to brush up on tech skills/knowledge to prepare for a 2nd attempt.
        • Must complete & pass the Tech Prep Workshop.
        • Will be made available via Full Sail One.
        • Once the Tech Prep Workshop is on file, the 2nd Attempt at the Learning Styles – Technology Assessment will be made available.
          • If a score of 0%-69% is received on the 2nd Attempt, students must wait 6 months before attempting the 3rd/Final Attempt.
            • If a score of 0%-69% is received on the 3rd/Final Attempt, entrance into a Full Sail Degree will not be permitted at this time.
      • 70% or higher = Admitted into program like regular.

  • Prior Offense Form
    • 1 Form per 1 Offense.
